Under /etc/udev/rules.d/99-luckfox-cams.rules create:

SUBSYSTEM=="video4linux", ATTRS{serial}=="123456", SYMLINK+="video-luckfox-front"
SUBSYSTEM=="video4linux", ATTRS{serial}=="654321", SYMLINK+="video-luckfox-back"

sudo udevadm control --reload-rules
sudo udevadm trigger

can be accessed with:
int fd = open("/dev/video-luckfox-front", O_RDWR);
